## Features 
##### 1. Login View with Google or Facebook
##### 2. Sign up with Google or Facebook
##### 3. Upload Image or Video 
##### 4. Logout Button 
##### 5. Account Home with Post Uploaded by You
##### 6. Search View with Animation
##### 7. Home View with all Posts by all Users
